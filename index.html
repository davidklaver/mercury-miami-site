Why Hire Us?

Professionalism 

At Mercury Maintenance we provide top of the line customer service. We have an owner present on nearly every job. We take pride in our quality work and make it a priority to be available for our customers, whether it’s a last-minute emergency or a special event.

Experience

We know windows! With over 50 years of experience, and a focus on staying current with innovation in equipment and techniques, we ensure that you get the best possible result.

Accountability

When you hire Mercury Maintenance you will be dealing directly with one of the owners. That means that your service man has skin in the game. This type of accountability translates to superior service. This is why so many of South Florida’s most prestigious showrooms entrust their windows to us.

About Our Company

Mercury Maintenance was founded in 1957 by Sidney, who learned the trade from his father, a window cleaner in New York City. Now in its 3rd generation, we are still family owned and operated, committed to servicing customers with integrity, quality and attention to detail.

Our biggest testimonial is our satisfied customers, most of whom we have been servicing for years. They know through experience that they can rely on us for honest, friendly and professional service.
